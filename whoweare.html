Who we are

Inspire Innovative Cohort is a technology driven product innovation and development team who deploys digital products to solve pressing needs experienced by persons and communities.

Our Objective is to improve human capacity for social impact.

A core part of our approach is to launch products that meet market needs through human centered design, improve learning capabilities in youngsters through our outreach program themed Entrepreneurship for social impact and Design thinking for problem solving.

Our Team

We are a league of highly motivated and inspired minds dedicated to deploying products that addresses painpoints in persons and in communities to market by leveraging on technology and human centred design approach.
